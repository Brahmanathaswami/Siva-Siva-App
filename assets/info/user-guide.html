User Guide

Exploring Images

In almost all contexts, try:

  • Tap once: The image will shrink to show all.

  • Tap again: The image is full size, and centered.

  • Hold down and drag: Scroll around to see the rest of the image.

  • Click on Nataraja icon: On the "home" screen. To see a different image of Lord Siva

Personalize SivaSiva

The Journal

There are many activities in SivaSiva App. We call the modules. To personalize:

  • Whenever you see the Above Icon

    It will save your current activity to the "Journal".

  • Tap to:

    • To save a module, such as "Word Puzzles" or "Hinduism Today" or a YouTube video that you to watch again on finish later when you have time.
    • To save an audio file. For example a long talk you want to go back to.
    • In "Gems" you can save a favorite quote to think about later.
    • In "Path to Siva" bookmark where left off.
  • Sometimes the "Journal" icon appears in the "Info" or "Settings" for a modules

Favorites

  • You have many things entered to your journal. Click the star on the left of an entry to make it a "Favorite".
  • Click on "Filter by Favorites" to show only "Favorites".
  • ""Favorites" will also appear as "My Menu" the upper left navigator, on the "home" screen.
