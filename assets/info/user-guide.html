User Guide

Exploring Images

In almost all contexts try:

  • Tap once: The image will shrink to show all.

  • Tap again: The image is full size and centered.

  • Hold down and drag: Scroll around to see the rest of the image.

  • Click on the Nataraja icon on the "portal" screen, to see a different image of Lord Siva

Personalize SivaSiva

The Journal

There are many activities in SivaSiva App. We call them modules. To personalize:

  • Whenever you see the above icon: It will save a link to that current activity in the "Journal".

  • Tap to:

    • To save a link to a module, such as "Word Puzzles" or "Hinduism Today" or a YouTube video that you want finish later when you have time.
    • To save a link to an audio file. For example, a long talk that you want to go back to later.
    • In "Gems" you can hit "heart" icon which saves your favorite to the "Journal".
    • In Path to Siva, bookmark where left off to the "Journal".
    • In the books Dancing with Siva, Living with Siva, Merging with Siva, or any publication that you go a "web" view, you can save the page your were on as the most recent bookmark.
    • Click on the yellow "gold coins" stack of the bottom of the home screen. If you are connected to the internet, this will bring up "Topics of Interest". Numerous items from our website can be explored.
    • When you go to a Topic of Interest, items are categorized as "audio, video, text". Any of these can be linked in the "Journal" for future reference. Items categorized as "text" will take you to the cover view. Underneath the cover, click "Read book now on the web" and you go to the inside of the book. Click the Journal icon when on any page, to bookmark it for future reading.
  • If you wish to delete items, click on the icon in upper corner that has a pencil. It will take you through icons with a red cross out. Click the red cross out to get rid of the extra bookmark. Click the left arrow the top to get back.

Favorites

  • Over time, you will have many things entered to your journal. Click the star on the left of an entry to make it a "Favorite".
  • Click on "Filter by Favorites" to show only "Favorites".
  • "Favorites" will also appear as "My Menu" in the upper left navigator.

Audio

  • Loading Audio: Go to "Listen". There will "collections" - various talks, music/songs, chants or audio books. Click one these. A list of titles will appear. Click title and then audio will play. A title will show up. Follow by lyrics or a transcripts if it exists. Otherwise a picture of Siva will appear.
  • In the lower screen, it shows you the duration of the audio, timestamp of where you are. You click on "Pause" or "Play"
  • Saving Audio to "My Downloads": "My Downloads" to play later. Use the small icon red icon in the upper corner of the lyrics display to downloads to the "My Downloads." Only those files which have been downloaded can save to the "Journal". You will be taken to the time place of file. For instance, if you listen to talk for 15 minutes, place it in the "Journal". Tomorrow, it will take to the talk at 15 minutes.
  • Periodically, to gain space on your phone, you can delete the audio files you don't need. Click the word "Delete" icon in lower right corner. You will be taken to a screen which shows you the file name of something you can delete. Click the lower if left arrow to go back to "My Downloads".
  • It is recommended that you are in a Wi-Fi area to download talks or audiobooks that will take lots of time. Or, if have a patience, you can do something that will to 15 minutes to download. Click on "Loading" the turn it off.
  • Speaker Icon: The audio is set to play while you go to other activities and other app. In the app, on other screens, a small speaker icon appears. If the audio is not playing, click it once to play. If audio is playing, click in once to end it.
  • We have lots of audio feature requests and are working to implement them over time. Please go to Feedback under settings and send us your suggestions. This best method to get back audio that you like: download to My Audio, then record your choices in the Journal.

My Data

  • SivaSiva may take up more memory as you download audio or other media.
  • Click on "My Data" to find out how much is used
