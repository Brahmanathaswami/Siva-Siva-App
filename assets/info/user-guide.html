User Guide

Exploring Images

In almost all contexts try:

  • Tap once: The image will shrink to show all.

  • Tap again: The image is full size and centered.

  • Hold down and drag: Scroll around to see the rest of the image.

  • Click on Nataraja icon: On the "home" screen to see a different image of Lord Siva

Personalize SivaSiva

The Journal

There are many activities in SivaSiva App. We call them modules. To personalize:

  • Whenever you see the above icon: It will save a link to your current activity in the "Journal".

  • Tap to:

    • To save a link to a module, such as "Word Puzzles" or "Hinduism Today" or a YouTube video that you to want finish later when you have time.
    • To save a link to an audio file. For example, a long talk that you want to go back to later.
    • In "Gems" you can save a line to a favorite quote to meditate on later.
    • In Path to Siva, bookmark where left off.
    • In the books Dancing with Siva, Living with Siva, Merging with Siva, the journal will only show one title but latest one is your most recent bookmark.
    • Click on the yellow "pancake" stack of the bottom of the home screen. If you are connected to the internet, this will bring up "Topics of Interest". Numerous items from our website can be explored.
  • Sometimes the "Journal" icon appears in the "Info" or "Settings" for a module.

Favorites

  • Over time, you will have many things entered to your journal. Click the star on the left of an entry to make it a "Favorite".
  • Click on "Filter by Favorites" to show only "Favorites".
  • "Favorites" will also appear as "My Menu" in the upper left navigator.

Audio

  • Loading Icon: The audio interface is a work in progress. We have included a new loading message. If you have low bandwidth, the loading message will stay on screen. Be patient, do not click on another talk until it is finished downloading. If your bandwidth is not good, you can click on the message and your download will end as stops connecting to the internet. Sometimes the loading message goes away, which means a successful downloaded, but there may be is silence until the audio or talk plays.
  • Saving Audio to My Audio: It is recommend to download the audio file when you have Wi-Fi access and then keep in My Audio to play later. Use the small icon on the bottom right to download any file that you are selected. Periodically, to gain space on your phone, you can delete the audio files you don't need. (see below)
  • Speaker Icon: The audio is set play while you go to other activities. A small speaker icon appear. If the audio is no playing click it once to play. If audio is playing, click in one to end it. We have lots or requests to make audio work better. Please you Feedback under settings and send use you suggestions. For now this best method to get back audio that you like, a) download to My Audio b) record your choices in the Journal.

My Data

  • SivaSiva may take up more memory as you download audio or other media.
  • Click on "My Data" to find out how much is used
  • Delete anything that you don't need to reduce the overall size that app takes on your phone.
