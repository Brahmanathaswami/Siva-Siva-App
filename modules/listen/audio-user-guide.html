Collections

Depending on your bandwidth, if you click on first two of Collections - Recent Additions and Bodhinathas Lastest - we go to the distance server. So it comes up "Loading Collection ......Tap to Stop". It may fast if you have WiFi. Or take time with cellular connection. Be patient! It will give you a list of titles. Or tap on "Stop".

Collections, for example, "Classical Artist", "Popular Tunes", "Ganesha Bhajans" will go to local database. They will not to take time to load the titles. But they will take time to load the title when you go the play it. For the songs which are short, they will be fast. For the songs which take 10 minutes or longer to play. .be patience. They will come up "Loading Collection ......Tap to Stop". Be patient. It will play.

Now, with cellular connection, if it takes time - 5-10 minutes, for example -- then tap to stop -- "Loading Collection ......Tap to Stop". Then go a "My Downloads" where you listen to a song, even a long talk, with no connection. And you can go to some feature which does require a cellular connect.

Download and Your Journal

If you have bandwidth, tap the Download icon in the upper right corner. You will come to "Cancel | Download". Tap on "Downloads". Wait until it goes away. Now your tap the Journal icon in lowest icon:

Saved to Journal

You can later go to "My Download". Tap, for example, a long to talk, you can listen to it for ten minutes. Tap the Journal icon ("Saved to Journal"). At a later time, you tap on Journal, tap the long talk, and it plays taking you to the 10 minutes where your listen before. Continue you listening, say, 10 minutes. Tap the Journal. You will get two Journal entries, the most recent at top. Tap on the top one. It will take you to 20 minute marker, and you listen from there. You could click the Journal icon at a place where you want to take time to "get it".

You can go to Journal. Tap on the Delete icon (upper right corner). It will take you to a X icon. Tap the lowest version of Journal entry. It will go away!
