There are two ways to read this book: from front to back like any other book, or by reading chapters related to topics that interest you.

At the top of most pages is a navigation bar with a Back arrow and a Table of contents icon. The Table of Contents lets you quickly jump from one page to another.

The Back arrow will return you to the Contents page you originally started from.

Photo Index

The Photo Index lists all chapters in order. Tap on any photo to read the chapter.

Exploring Realms

Exploring Realms displays a map. Each location on the map will show chapters related to a particular topic.

Reading Chapter pages

Turn pages by swiping left or right, or by tapping at the left or right edges of the body text.

Tap anywhere near the center of the body text to display the top navigation bar where you can access the Back arrow or the Table of Contents.

Every chapter has an image with a caption. Tap on the image or on the colored bar under it to read the caption. Tap again to hide the caption.

Long-press a word in the text to see its definition. If the word is in the glossary its definition will be shown.

Use the Table of Contents to move to pages that aren't chapters, such as the Glossary or this About page. The Home menu item takes you back to the main Read portal.

Glossary

Glossary definitions sometimes contain other terms also in the glossary. Tap a word in the definition to jump to it.
