There are two ways to read this book: from front to back like any other book, or by reading lessons related to topics that interest you.

At the top of most pages is a navigation bar with a Back arrow and a Table of Contents icon. The Table of Contents lets you quickly move from one page to another.

The Back arrow will return you to the Index page you started from.

Photo Index

The Photo Index lists all lessons in order. Tap on any photo to read the lesson.

Exploring Realms

Exploring Realms displays a map. Each location on the map will show lessons related to a particular topic.

Reading Lessons

Turn pages by swiping left or right, or by tapping at the left or right edges of the text.

On lesson pages the naviagion bar is normally hidden. Tap anywhere near the center of the text to show the top navigation bar and access the Back arrow or the Table of Contents. If you change your mind, tap the text again to hide the navigation bar.

Every lesson has an image with a caption. Tap the image or the colored bar under it to read the caption. Tap again to hide the caption.

Long-press a word in the text to see its definition. If the word is in the glossary its definition will be shown.

Use the Table of Contents to move to other sections of the book, such as the Glossary or this About page. The Home menu item takes you back to the main Read portal in the app.

Glossary

Glossary definitions sometimes contain other terms also in the glossary. You can tap a word in a definition to see that word's definition.
