Stories
User Guide

Currently we have stories made from five slide shows, based on random verses from different scriptures.

Clicking the back button takes you to the menu, clicking again takes you to the Portal Navigation.

As long as you keep SivaSiva open (even if you go to another app) the Stories module remembers where you are and will come back, so you can continue where you left off.

If you quit that app, or click on another story, then you would start at the beginning of a story.

Clicking on the journal will remember the story you are on, and the scene. Even if you quit the app.

You can drag an image back and forth and up and down to see the full image.
